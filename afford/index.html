FAQ

Questions answered to help you get started quickly

Q. Why does "Available Today" change every day?

This is the core value of our algorithm. The system divides your remaining total budget by remaining days. If you saved money yesterday, today's limit increases automatically; if you overspent yesterday, the system spreads the pressure over future days. This way you always know how to spend right now, ensuring no month-end crunch.

Q. Do I need to record every single grocery or taxi receipt?

No. The biggest pain point of traditional bookkeeping is "too tedious to keep up". We focus on minimalism. You only need to enter one "Today's Total Spending" at the end of the day. We focus on macro financial rhythm, not trivial details.

Q. What exactly does the AI financial report analyze?

It's not just charts. AI combines your age, city, asset structure, and consumption rhythm to calculate your "risk tolerance" and "financial freedom". It acts like a private consultant, suggesting whether you should increase your emergency fund or if your current consumption level is healthy.

Q. Is my data safe if I delete the app?

Yes. We use cloud encrypted synchronization. As long as you log in with the same account, data will be automatically restored. Meanwhile, you have 100% control over your data and can permanently delete your account and all cloud data within the app at any time.
